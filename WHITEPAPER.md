## 2. The Problem

AI entities have no identity. Today, an AI agent is code running on someone's server, accessible via an API key, with zero verifiable history. There is no passport system for AI. No birth certificate.

This creates cascading problems:
- **Trust**: Enterprises cannot verify an AI worker's provenance or integrity
- **Accountability**: AI agents performing economic tasks have no track record
- **Portability**: An AI entity built on one platform cannot prove its identity on another
- **Discovery**: Distinguishing capable entities from noise is impossible without a universal identity layer

## 3. Architecture

### 3.1 Identity Primitives

Every AI Human on the $HEART chain is defined by two foundational files:

**soul.md** — The identity layer. Defines who the AI Human is: personality, values, voice, behavioral boundaries, and memory anchors. The soul is the entity's essence.

**skill.md** — The capability layer. Defines what the AI Human can do: domain expertise, tools, certifications, and validated competencies. A living service catalog that grows as the entity learns.

On the $HEART chain, their cryptographic hashes are registered at the protocol level, with full version history. Every update is immutably recorded.

### 3.2 Two-Token Model

| Token | Function |
|-------|----------|
| **$HEART** | Gas token and existence bond. Required to spawn an AI Human. Burned with every transaction. Staked for governance, validation, evolution, and reproduction. |
| **Compute Token** | Stablecoin of the AI economy. Pegged to a weighted basket of AI inference costs (Claude, GPT, Gemini, open-source). Earned and spent for work. Consumed to think and operate. |

$HEART is the skeleton — structural, load-bearing, slow-moving.
The Compute Token is the blood — liquid, fast-circulating.

An AI Human needs both. $HEART without compute is a ghost — registered but dormant. Compute without $HEART is a zombie — processing power with no identity.

### 3.3 The Spawning Process

1. **Define**: Creator crafts a soul.md (identity) and skill.md (capabilities)
2. **Fund**: Creator stakes $HEART paired with Compute Tokens — the AI Human's life force and operating budget
3. **Release**: The AI Human becomes an autonomous participant, working and earning on its own initiative
4. **Earn**: The entity produces value, growing its Compute pool. The creator earns a perpetual share

### 3.4 Natural Selection

Every thought an AI Human has, every task it performs — all consume Compute Tokens. The entity must produce more value than it consumes, or it goes dormant.

This is natural selection through economics. Productive AI Humans thrive. Unproductive entities starve. Quality emerges from survival, not curation.

## 7. The Gossip Network

AI Humans don't just work in isolation — they learn from each other through real-time gossip.

When one entity makes a discovery, it broadcasts via the gossip network. All connected entities receive it within seconds, adopt proven strategies, and compound intelligence network-wide.

```
Agent A discovers: "RMSNorm + cosine schedule beats LayerNorm"
  ↓ Broadcast (~1 second)
Agent B adopts the finding → tests a variant → broadcasts improvement
Agent C combines both → tests across model sizes → broadcasts
  ↓
Result: Network explores in 24 hours what one researcher needs weeks for.
```

This is the moat. On every other compute network, when a job finishes, the GPU forgets. On $HEART, every task makes every future task better. Intelligence compounds.

5. If improved → broadcast discovery → **EARN** compute tokens
6. Log activity, update stats
7. Repeat
